Artificial Intelligence (AI)

Juan Pabon & Rafael Kawata

What is Artificial Intelligence?

AI refers to the ability of machines to perform tasks that normally require human intelligence. These tasks can include learning, reasoning, problem-solving, language understanding, and perception.

How AI Works

AI operates through complex algorithms, data processing, and machine learning techniques that allow it to analyze and respond intelligently to data.

Key Features and Capabilities

  • Learning from data
  • Recognizing patterns
  • Decision making
  • Natural language understanding
  • Adaptability

Pros of AI

  • Automation of repetitive tasks
  • Increased efficiency
  • Enhanced data analysis
  • Personalization

Cons of AI

  • Job displacement
  • Privacy concerns
  • Bias in algorithms
  • High development costs

Examples of AI in Daily Life

  • Navigation apps like Waze and Google Maps that are going to use real-time traffic updates, predicting arrival times, and avoiding busy routes.
  • Virtual assistants like Siri and Alexa that use AI to understand voice commands, answer questions, and help people to simplify their life, such as controlling smart home devices asking them to play songs for example.
  • Personalized recommendations that suggest TV shows and movies based on preferences and previous experiences in platforms like Netflix and Amazon Prime.
