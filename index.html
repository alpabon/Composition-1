Artificial Intelligence (AI)

Juan Pabon & Rafael Kawata

What is Artificial Intelligence?

AI refers to the ability of machines to perform tasks that normally require human intelligence. These tasks can include learning, reasoning, problem-solving, language understanding, and perception.

How AI Works

AI operates through complex algorithms, data processing, and machine learning techniques that allow it to analyze and respond intelligently to data.

Key Features and Capabilities

  • Learning from data
  • Recognizing patterns
  • Decision making
  • Natural language understanding
  • Adaptability

Pros of AI

  • Automation of repetitive tasks
  • Increased efficiency
  • Enhanced data analysis
  • Personalization

Cons of AI

  • Job displacement
  • Privacy concerns
  • Bias in algorithms
  • High development costs

Examples of AI in Daily Life

  • Navigation apps like Waze and Google Maps that use real-time traffic updates and predict arrival times.
  • Virtual assistants like Siri and Alexa that help simplify daily life and control smart home devices.
  • Personalized recommendations on platforms like Netflix and Amazon Prime.
