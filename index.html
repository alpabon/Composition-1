Artificial Intelligence (AI)

Juan Pabon & Rafael Kawata

What is Artificial Intelligence?

AI refers to the ability of machines to perform tasks that normally require human intelligence. These tasks can include learning, reasoning, problem-solving, language understanding, and perception.

How AI Works

Artificial Intelligence or AI works by making computers do jobs that usually require human intelligence. AI operates through algorithms and networks that process a huge amount of data, identifying patterns and correlations to provide solutions and make life easier.

Key Features and Capabilities

  • Learning from data
  • Natural language processing
  • Predictive analytics
  • Computer vision
  • Adaptability

Pros of AI

  • Eliminates human error and risk
  • 24/7 availability
  • Unbiased decision-making
  • Cost reduction
  • Data acquisition and analysis

Cons of AI

  • Costly implementation
  • Lack of emotion and creativity
  • Degradation
  • Reduced jobs for humans
  • Ethical problems

Examples of AI in Daily Life

  • Navigation apps like Waze and Google Maps that use real-time traffic updates and predict arrival times.
  • Virtual assistants like Siri and Alexa that help simplify daily life and control smart home devices.
  • Personalized recommendations on platforms like Netflix and Amazon Prime.
